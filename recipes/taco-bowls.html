Soft Potato Taco Bowls

Because you can never go wrong with mexican food

Ingredients

Taco Bowls

  • 2-3 russet potatoes
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • Seasonings
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste

Pico

  • 3 tomatoes
  • 2 jalepe√±os
  • Purple onion
  • Cilantro

Toppings

  • Sour cream or greek yogurt
  • Guac
  • Cheese
  • Lime
Steps
  1. Scub potatoes and chop into cubes. Drizzle with olive oil and all purpose seasoning (like Tony Chachere's). Bake at 425 degrees for 30 mins.
  2. Make the pico by chopping up all ingredients and combine.
  3. Heat a skillet on medium and when warm, add ground beef cooking until browned. Drain fat then add seasonings, tomato paste and a couple tbsp of water.
  4. Combine all ingredients in a bowl and enjoy!
